# Launch worship sub-brand channel

## Purpose

Stand up **Union Sound** or **Elevation Worship** as a distinct channel without splitting church narrative.

## Deliverables

- Avatar, banner, bio from sub-brand tokens
- 4-week launch content plan (release, BTS, live clip, church cross-promo)
- Token inheritance verified via `brand-system.logos.define_subbrand_inheritance`

## Rules

- Sub-brand posts ≤ 25% of master church calendar
- Link back to church profile in bio
- Worship CCLI compliance on all lyrics clips
