# Share Your Story intake form and consent

## Purpose

Standardize testimony collection with **consent**, **review checklist**, and brand-safe prompts.

## Intake prompts (member-facing)

1. What brought you to Faith Chapel?
2. What changed in your life through faith or community?
3. One sentence you'd tell a friend about this church.
4. Photo upload (optional, high resolution only — per brand forbidden list).

## Consent (required)

- I grant Faith Chapel permission to use my story, name, and photo on web, social, and service media.
- I understand I may request removal by contacting [comms email].

## Reviewer checklist

- [ ] Story aligns with values in profile
- [ ] No unverified medical/financial claims
- [ ] Photo resolution acceptable (no clipart/low-res)
