# Outreach impact feature

## Purpose

Show **community impact** — local outreach, Bridge events, global missions — as story with dignity (not poverty tourism).

## Steps

1. Partner with outreach lead for approved metrics and quotes.
2. Photo: volunteers + community with consent; avoid exploitative framing.
3. Formats: carousel (3–5 slides), web feature, one service slide optional.
4. CTA: serve / give / next outreach signup.

## Profile hooks

- Faith Chapel: **The Bridge**
- Union: local MD/VA/NC campus outreach
- Highlands: **Make a Difference**
- Elevation: Outreach Ministry + eFam tie-in when relevant
