# Build community campaign not event flyer

## Purpose

Modern church media tells **who we are over time** — a 4–6 week narrative arc, not a single date graphic.

## Campaign arc template

| Week | Beat | Asset type |
|------|------|------------|
| 1 | Why we care | Values / pastor vision (short) |
| 2 | Who we are | Community faces, stories |
| 3 | What we're doing | Serve, groups, Bridge, outreach |
| 4 | Join us | CTA — visit, group, serve |
| 5–6 | Stories + proof | Testimonies, impact |

## Decision rules

- Max 1 "event date only" graphic in arc; rest must carry **story**
- Use `church.story.balance_pastor_vs_community_faces` across arc
- Union/Highlands/Elevation: seeker-first copy on week 4 CTA
