# eFam community story

## Purpose

Tell **eFam** as distributed church — homes, watch parties, cities — not only campus Sunday.

## Story angles

- First-time host
- Multi-generational living room
- Pop-Up city gathering
- "Church where you are" testimony

## Deliverables

- Community story template (member/host face)
- CTA: eFam signup / Watch Party / Pop-Up info from elevationchurch.org
- Clip optional: host reaction to sermon moment (15s)

## Rules

- Pastor clip ≤ 1 in 4 eFam posts in a month
- Always show **local faces** (host, guests, neighborhood)
