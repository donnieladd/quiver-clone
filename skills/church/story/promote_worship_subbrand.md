# Promote worship sub-brand without overshadowing church

## Purpose

**Union Sound** and **Elevation Worship** deserve dedicated creative — but church narrative stays primary in the master calendar.

## Rules

- Sub-brand posts ≤ 25% of weekly social (church account)
- Always one church-story post between two worship promos
- Use sub-brand lockups from profile `sub_brands`
- Union: `church.worship.union_sound_release_kit` for releases
- Elevation: Open Network worship kit + link to elevationworship.com

## Deliverables

- Release/single graphic set
- 1 church cross-promo ("Our church family worships with…")
