# Balance four mission pillars in content calendar

## Purpose

Highlands content must reflect **Know God · Find Freedom · Discover Purpose · Make a Difference** — not one pillar dominating.

## Target mix (30-day rolling)

| Pillar | Target share |
|--------|--------------|
| Know God | 25–35% |
| Find Freedom | 20–30% |
| Discover Purpose | 15–25% |
| Make a Difference | 20–30% |

## Steps

1. Tag each scheduled post with primary pillar.
2. Calculate share; flag if any pillar <15% or >40%.
3. Rebalance with community story, groups, serve, or sermon clip skills.
