# Playbook: Transformation Church

**Lead Pastor:** Michael (Mike) Todd · **Church:** [Transformation Church](https://transformchurch.us/) · **Visual reference:** [OneDiversified case study](https://onediversified.com/transformation-church)

## What this playbook represents

Transformation Church is the **cinematic culture + pastor-as-human + iconic series IP** model. Media is designed for **global content ministry** — room and YouTube are one pipeline. This is the closest reference to Donnie's **future-forward, iconic** visual lens in the church space.

**Profile:** [`profiles/transformation-church.json`](../profiles/transformation-church.json)

## What they optimize for

1. **Excellence as worship** — technology and craft serve the message at broadcast scale
2. **Pictures over words** — sermon moments designed as visual memory ([Mike Todd on Deep End / Crosswalk](https://www.crosswalk.com/headlines/contributors/milton-quintanilla/mike-todd-opens-up-about-his-upbringing-and-addresses-recent-sermon-backlash.html))
3. **Memorable language** — vision phrases filter every touchpoint ("RE-present," not "represent")
4. **Pastor humanity on social** — phone-native BTS for relatability; cinematic for stage ([Pro Church Tools](https://prochurchtools.com/blog-post/how-pastor-mike-todd-dominates-social-media))
5. **Clip-first preaching** — illustrations built to survive as short-form clips

## Org model (media-relevant)

```
Lead Pastor (vision + personal brand channel)
    ├── Content Creator to Lead Pastor (social strategy 1:1)
    ├── Broadcast / cinematic production (sanctuary + stream)
    ├── Motion + stage design (immersive environments)
    └── Social team (Mike + church accounts coordinated)
```

**Skill implication:** Separate **stage cinematic** specs from **phone social** specs in same series.

## Story model — pastor-forward but dimensional

| Channel | Balance |
|---------|---------|
| Stage / YouTube | Cinematic, illustration-heavy, high production |
| Mike Todd Instagram | Personal, family, fitness, faith — **phone native** |
| Church social | Vision language + series + clips — not one-dimensional |
| Clips | Primary growth engine — designed at sermon time |

**Profile limit:** `pastor_face_ratio_max_30d: 0.55` — higher than Union/Faith Chapel because **pastor brand is distribution**, but still use **`church.story.apply_represent_language_filter`** so posts aren't ego-only.

## Visual / motion direction

- Bold color, eye-catching series art (Behance brand identity reference)
- Immersive lighting, motion backgrounds, IMAG-aware design
- Same excellence bar online and in-room
- Series identity persists across weeks — **iconic, slow to expire**

## Asset cadence

| Rhythm | Assets |
|--------|--------|
| Weekly | Full service video, 5–15 clips, quote graphics, stories |
| Series | Cinematic key art, motion set, opener, thumbnails, stage |
| Pastor personal | Phone reels, BTS, family — parallel calendar |
| Drops | Merch, podcast, study content tied to series IP |

## Steal / avoid

| Steal | Avoid |
|-------|-------|
| Clip-first sermon writing | Static sermon slides with no clip potential |
| Vision phrase on every post | Generic church graphics with no language system |
| Phone social for pastor humanity | Pro camera for fake-casual Instagram |
| Cinematic series kits | Weekly redesign with no series equity |
| Global stream parity | In-room-only design that fails on YouTube thumbnail |
