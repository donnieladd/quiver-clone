# Playbook: Elevation Church

**Lead Pastor:** Steven Furtick · **Church:** [Elevation Church](https://www.elevationchurch.org/) · **Resources:** [Open Network partner](https://open.life.church/partners/elevation-church) · **Pastor platform:** [Steven Furtick Ministries](https://stevenfurtick.com/)

## What this playbook represents

Elevation is the **scale + distributed church + pastor clip engine + Open Network generosity** model. Creative quality is broadcast-grade; reach is multi-modal (campus, online, watch party, pop-up).

**Profile:** [`profiles/elevation-church.json`](../profiles/elevation-church.json)

## What they optimize for

1. **Mission** — "People far from God will be raised to life in Christ"
2. **Distributed experiences** — Physical campus · Live stream (eFam) · Watch Party · Pop-Up ([elevationchurch.org](https://www.elevationchurch.org/))
3. **Pastor clip distribution** — 1-minute clips, YouTube, email devotionals ([stevenfurtick.com](https://stevenfurtick.com/))
4. **Open Network kits** — PSD, AI, MP4 series drops for global church use
5. **Creative pipeline** — Paid creative apprenticeship; mentor-driven portfolio growth

## Experience map (media-relevant)

| Mode | Story / media angle |
|------|---------------------|
| **Physical campus** | Local community, serve, groups |
| **eFam (online)** | Same weekend, digital congregation |
| **Watch Party** | Small-group streaming — community in homes |
| **Pop-Up** | City-tour energy — event + invite creative |
| **Steven Furtick YT/clips** | Pastor teaching moments as growth engine |

## Story model — clips + community + scale

| Channel | Balance |
|---------|---------|
| Sermon / series | High polish; Open Network kit export |
| Steven Furtick clips | Short motivational/teaching clips |
| Church social | eGroups, outreach, worship, invite — not pastor-only |
| Watch Party / eFam | Community hosts as story subjects |

**Profile limit:** `pastor_face_ratio_max_30d: 0.5` — clips are strategic but **eGroups/outreach** must show faces beyond Steven.

## Series kit (Open Network pattern)

Elevation drops on Open Network include: message series, social artwork, video creatives, titles, logos, splash screens ([partner page](https://open.life.church/partners/elevation-church)).

**Skills:** `church.series.package_open_network_style_kit` + `church.series.ship_open_network_elevation_kit`

## Steal / avoid

| Steal | Avoid |
|-------|-------|
| Full Open Network zip per series | Single JPG with no kit |
| Watch Party promo templates | Ignoring distributed viewers |
| Clip-first Steven Furtick pipeline | Only long-form YouTube with no shorts |
| Creative apprenticeship job descriptions | Undefined creative roles |
| Pop-up city creative as invite story | Generic nationwide stock without local hook |
