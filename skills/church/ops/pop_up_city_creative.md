# Pop-Up city creative

## Purpose

**Pop-Up Church** city events — bold local creative that feels like Elevation energy with city-specific pride.

## Asset set

- Key visual: city name + date (Elevation typography)
- Countdown series (7, 3, 1 day)
- Post-event: community faces recap (not pastor-only stage shot)

## Steps

1. Confirm city name spelling + legal with comms team.
2. Use profile brand colors; optional city skyline **subtle** (not cliché overload).
3. Localize CTA for registration URL.
4. Archive with **`church.story.archive_published_stories`**.
