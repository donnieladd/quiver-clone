# Select platform for objective

## Purpose

**One decision** — channel before you write a word or generate a pixel.

## Objective → platform matrix

| Objective | Primary platforms | Content shape | AI role |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------|---------|
| B2B pipeline / ABM | LinkedIn, email, sales deck | Thought leadership, case studies | LLM drafts; human exec voice |
| Product launch (PMM) | Landing page, Product Hunt, email, paid | Launch narrative, demo clips | Higgsfield product shots; HeyGen demo |
| DTC / ecommerce | Meta, TikTok Shop, Amazon A+ | UGC-style, product loops | Seedream/Nano product; Quiver icons |
| Luxury fashion drop | Instagram, TikTok, email, lookbook PDF | Editorial stills, 9:16 film | Soul moodboards; human shoot for hero |
| SEO / editorial scale | Blog, newsletter, YouTube | Long-form, clusters, repurposed clips | Outline + draft; fact-check gate |
| Personal executive brand | LinkedIn, podcast, speaking | Essays, clips, headshots | LLM ghost; real photo only |
| Creator monetization | YouTube, Substack, Patreon | Series, community posts | Media engine for thumbs + b-roll |
| Church / ministry | YouTube, Instagram, email | Sermon clips, invite cards | Quiver SVG; brand tokens |

## Rules

1. **One primary channel per campaign** — others are repurposed, not co-primary
2. **Match aspect ratio at generation** — use `media.router` presets per platform
3. **Luxury + corporate**: never lead with AI faces in hero assets
4. **Editorial**: AI assists research/outline; publish requires citation pass
