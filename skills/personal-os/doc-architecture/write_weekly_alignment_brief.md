# Write weekly alignment brief

## Purpose

15-min reset: intention, top 3, constraints, energy forecast. Not a todo list.

## Personal template (Sunday night — 15 min max)

- **Week of:** (Mon–Sun starting tomorrow)
- **Energy:** low / medium / high
- **One-line intention:** Why this week matters in one sentence
- **Top 3 outcomes:** (not 15 tasks — outcomes)
- **Constraints:** time, money, appointments, travel
- **Protect:** one thing that doesn't get sacrificed
- **Park lot:** ideas to concept-note later — do not chase tonight
