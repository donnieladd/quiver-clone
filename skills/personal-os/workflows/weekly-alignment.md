# Workflow: Weekly personal alignment

Sunday night, 15 minutes: alignment brief + scorecard + next week top 3. Your home rhythm.

## When to use

- Every Sunday night (~15 min)
- Feeling scattered or reactivity-driven

## When NOT to use

- Mid-crisis — stabilize first, align after

## Primary platforms

Personal OS · Notes app

## Steps

### Step 1 — Honest energy check

**Type:** `input`

Low / medium / high — plan capacity, not fantasy.

**Inputs:**
- Energy this week (low/med/high) (required)
- What drained you?

### Step 2 — Personal scorecard

**Type:** `skill`
**Skill:** `personal-os.doc.write_personal_scorecard`

5 lead + 5 lag — numbers or honest ranges, not guilt.

### Step 3 — Weekly alignment brief

**Type:** `skill`
**Skill:** `personal-os.doc.write_weekly_alignment_brief`

One intention, top 3 outcomes, constraints (time, money, people).

### Step 4 — Lock top 3

**Type:** `input`

undefined

**Inputs:**
- Top priority 1 (required)
- Top priority 2 (required)
- Top priority 3

### Step 5 — Non-negotiable SOPs check

**Type:** `checklist`

undefined

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Meds / health anchor scheduled
- [ ] Money touchpoint day set
- [ ] Sleep window protected
- [ ] One social/connect touch planned


## Outputs

- [ ] Alignment brief saved
- [ ] Scorecard updated
- [ ] Top 3 visible somewhere you'll see daily
