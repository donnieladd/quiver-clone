# Workflow: Life project charter

Before big personal commitments: scope, out-of-scope, success criteria, risks. Same brain you used for big days — for your life.

## When to use

- ≥4 hours/week for 2+ weeks
- ≥$500 or high emotional load
- Move, launch, health protocol, major relationship conversation prep

## When NOT to use

- Small errands
- Already in weekly top 3

## Primary platforms

Personal OS

**Type:** `skill`

### Step 4 — Future You sign-off

**Type:** `gate`

Would calm-you on a good day approve this scope?

**Gate criteria:**
- Scope fits actual capacity
- Out-of-scope written
- First next action < 30 min
